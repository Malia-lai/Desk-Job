<h1>Desk Job</h1>
<p>A 2D top-down RPG about a man trapped in the monotony of office life. Once a passionate artist, he gave up his dreams for stability. Now he spends his days pulling pranks on coworkers, trying to bring color back into a life that has turned completely gray. As things spiral out of control, he begins to realize the pranks were never really about making people laugh — they were his way of escaping a routine that was slowly consuming him.</p>

<br />
<h2>How to Play</h2>
<p>Use <strong>WASD</strong> to move around the office. Press <strong>E</strong> to interact with coworkers and objects. Talk to everyone around you — NPCs react to what you do. Follow the story through the diary entries as the character slowly confronts what he has been avoiding. The game is short and story-driven, so take your time reading.</p>
